Seize the day. AND Let it be.

Posted by Dankforth Anchor

Seize the day

AND

Let it be.

Spread down, extend the roots

and reach up the branches,

bring birds and squirrels

in cahoots,

just like

a wildly planted

sturdy, broad, and tall

oak tree.
